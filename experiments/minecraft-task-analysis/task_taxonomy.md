# Minecraft Task Taxonomy

## Resource Collection

- Collect wood
- Mine stone
- Mine coal
- Mine iron
- Collect food

## Crafting

- Craft planks
- Craft crafting table
- Craft sticks
- Craft tools
- Craft furnace

## Survival

- Avoid hostile mobs
- Maintain hunger
- Build shelter
- Cook food

## Exploration

- Locate caves
- Navigate biomes
- Return to base
- Track landmarks

## Long-Horizon Goals

- Obtain iron pickaxe
- Enter the Nether
- Brew potions
- Defeat the Ender Dragon
